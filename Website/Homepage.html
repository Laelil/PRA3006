Welcome to our platform!
We have compiled a tool that aids in discovering various facts and figures regarding Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD).
This condition has been described as "the complex somatic, cognitive, affective, and behavioral effects of psychological trauma", and is linked to be caused by a myriad of traumatic experiences. Intrusive thoughts, nightmares, and hypervigilance are a few examples of consequences that characterize PTSD.

Our research is directed towards identifying the interactions between known PTSD treatment drugs and their target proteins.
